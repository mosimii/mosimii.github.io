About

I’m a Data & AI professional with a background in Geology who enjoys taking messy, real-world data and turning it into clear, useful products. I’ve built end-to-end solutions across several domains, including environmental and public-health projects (legionella risk monitoring, seismic data quality), computer vision (smart recycling, histopathology triage), and NLP / LLM work (bot detection, semantic recommendation systems).

My focus is on designing pipelines, models and interfaces that people can actually use: analysis-ready data, explainable results and simple UIs that help non-technical stakeholders make decisions. I enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams to ship practical, reliable data and AI tools.
